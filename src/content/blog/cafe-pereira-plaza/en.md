**Coffee at Pereira Plaza** is where the Café Los Grisales offer takes its most complete form. Our Pereira Plaza shop is not simply a place to sell coffee — it is a gathering point where specialty coffee culture becomes accessible, approachable, and enjoyable for every kind of customer in the city.

Pereira Plaza is one of the landmarks of commerce and entertainment in Pereira, and our presence there is both a strategic and philosophical decision. We want the finest coffee from the Eje Cafetero to be available exactly where the city lives and moves, not tucked away in an exclusive space that requires a special trip.

## Our shop at Pereira Plaza

The Pereira Plaza shop was designed with a clear intention: to create a space that speaks the same language as the coffee we serve. The materials, colors, and layout echo the landscape of the Eje Cafetero — wood, earthy tones, and the green-and-gold palette are all conscious references to the coffee fields of our region.

The bar is the central element of the space. It is a stage where baristas work in full view, with the espresso equipment, grinders, and filter methods within eyeshot. This transparency is deliberate: it is an invitation to curiosity. If you watch your barista weigh the coffee before grinding, hear them talk about the current harvest lot, or notice the precision with which they control extraction time, you are witnessing firsthand the standard that sets specialty coffee apart from mass-market alternatives.

## The experience

Visiting our Pereira Plaza shop is a sensory experience that starts before you even order. From the entrance, the aroma of freshly roasted coffee or an ongoing extraction sets the tone. It is an olfactory cue that shapes your expectation positively — what is coming is going to be good.

Service is an integral part of the experience. Our team at Pereira Plaza is trained to welcome every kind of guest, from the expert who arrives with technical questions to the curious first-timer who has only ever drunk instant coffee. There is no hierarchy at the bar and no condescension at the counter. Every person who walks in deserves to know what they are holding in their hands.

We periodically organize events in the space: open cuppings, presentations of special seasonal lots, and barismo workshops where guests can explore different brewing methods. These events are a natural extension of the bean-to-cup concept — education is part of the experience.

## Menu recommendations

The menu at our Pereira Plaza shop evolves with the harvest and available lots. That said, some preparations form the core of the Café Los Grisales experience:

**Single-origin espresso:** The foundation of our offer. An espresso prepared with beans from our Eje Cafetero farm, extracted with precision to honor the lot's profile. The coffee we are using changes month by month — ask the barista what we are serving today and what notes to expect.

**Cortado or specialty flat white:** If you prefer espresso with a touch of milk, these preparations keep coffee as the protagonist. The milk complements without dominating.

**Filter methods:** V60, Chemex, Aeropress, or whatever method we have available that day. These preparations let you explore the nuances of origin with greater openness. Ideal for a more leisurely visit.

**Seasonal drinks:** Preparations designed to reflect the time of year and available lots. These might include cold brew, preparations with more pronounced floral or fruity notes, or combinations featuring local ingredients.
